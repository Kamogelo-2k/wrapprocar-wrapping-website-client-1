FAQ

Pricing Questions

Why do prices vary by vehicle size?

+

Larger vehicles require more vinyl material and additional labour time. A hatchback uses significantly less film than an SUV or luxury sedan.

How does the R250 booking fee work?

+

When you book online, you pay a R250 non-refundable fee via PayPal to secure your appointment. This amount is deducted from your final invoice. Your project deposit (25–50% depending on service) is arranged separately after we confirm your quote.

What's included in the project deposit?

+

The project deposit secures materials and your installation slot. Full wraps require 50%; partial wraps and PPF require 25%. The balance is due on completion.

Do specialty finishes cost more?

+

Yes. Chrome, carbon fibre, and satin finishes use premium materials and may add 5–50% to the base price depending on your selection in the quote builder.

Can I get a quote for fleet vehicles?

+

Absolutely. Fleet and commercial branding projects receive custom volume pricing. Contact us with your fleet size for a tailored package.
